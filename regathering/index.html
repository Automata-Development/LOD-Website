Regathering Info

 

We are so excited to welcome you back to in-person worship services at Light of the Desert!

Our gatherings will continue to be welcoming and friendly, but we’ll be taking some extra precautions to ensure that our LOD family stays safe.

Health & Safety

We are taking some extra precautions to ensure the safety of our LOD family:

 
* We strongly recommend you wear a mask when you attend in-person worship. If you do not wear a mask, we will assume you have a health condition that prevents you from wearing a mask but we will not ask you about your health condition.
 
 

To help ensure the safety of those around you, please join us online and do not come to in-person worship if you:

  • Feel sick or have had a fever in the past 24 hours
  • Have had contact with someone who has COVID-19
  • Have any underlying health conditions
  • Consider yourself to be a vulnerable or “at-risk” part of the population
Children

Children are to stay with their families during our intergenerational, “family style” gatherings. We will not offer Sunday School at this time, but there will be coloring tables set up at the back of the room for children to use during the service. We will have a “cry room” available in the lobby where parents will be able to watch the service on a TV screen.
